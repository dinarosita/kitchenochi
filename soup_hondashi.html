Soup

Hondasi based

About:

A quick random home cooking with whatever ingredients found in pantry and fridge. Ingredients:
  • 1 onion, cut
  • 5 garlic cloves, hit
  • 3-5 green onion
  • a bunch of cilantro leaves

  • 1 tbs oil
  • 6 cups of water

  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 1-2 carrots, cube
  • 1-2 potatoes, cube

  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 125 gr ground meat
  • 2 layers of fish cakes
  • 1/2 box of tofu
  • 6 mushrooms

  • 3 tsp hondashi
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 0.5 tsp msg
  • 0.5 tsp beef powder
Directions:
  1. Boil eggs.
  2. Fry onion, garlic, then ground beef.
  3. Add water.
  4. Add all cut up ingredients.
  5. Add seasoning.
  6. Taste, continue 10 minutes.
Serve with:
  • Rice
  • Chili
Notes:

The seasoning amount is good for 1.5 liter of water. However there was not enough broth.
